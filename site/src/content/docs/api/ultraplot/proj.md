`ultraplot.proj`

Additional cartopy projection classes.

## Public Classes

### `Aitoff(_WarpedRectangularProjection)`

The `Aitoff <https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Aitoff_projection>`__ projection.

### `Hammer(_WarpedRectangularProjection)`

The `Hammer <https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hammer_projection>`__ projection.

### `KavrayskiyVII(_WarpedRectangularProjection)`

The `Kavrayskiy VII <https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Kavrayskiy_VII_projection>`__ projection.

### `WinkelTripel(_WarpedRectangularProjection)`

The `Winkel tripel (Winkel III) <https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Winkel_tripel_projection>`__ projection.

### `NorthPolarAzimuthalEquidistant(AzimuthalEquidistant)`

Analogous to `~cartopy.crs.NorthPolarStereo`.

### `SouthPolarAzimuthalEquidistant(AzimuthalEquidistant)`

Analogous to `~cartopy.crs.SouthPolarStereo`.

### `NorthPolarLambertAzimuthalEqualArea(LambertAzimuthalEqualArea)`

Analogous to `~cartopy.crs.NorthPolarStereo`.

### `SouthPolarLambertAzimuthalEqualArea(LambertAzimuthalEqualArea)`

Analogous to `~cartopy.crs.SouthPolarStereo`.

### `NorthPolarGnomonic(Gnomonic)`

Analogous to `~cartopy.crs.NorthPolarStereo`.

### `SouthPolarGnomonic(Gnomonic)`

Analogous to `~cartopy.crs.SouthPolarStereo`.
